Ideation Questions

    What is the name of your startup?

    Lets Play Flag!

    What collaborative activity can people do together using your app?

    People can use this app to sign up to play flag football with random people to make a team play whenever!

    What is the ideal number of concurrent users?

    Between 7 and 11 people per team so between 14-22 people on the field and maybe some subs so a max of 28 people.

    What skills do people need in order to do this activity?

    No real skills needed, but the basic skills of understanding the fundamentals of football

    Is this activity for fun or for something practical?

    This is a fun activity for anyone to play

    How can a person see what everyone else is doing?

    As soon as someone signs up anyone can see the new applicates form

    How can a person see the most recent result of everyones collaborative effort?

    When someone signs up there form is show and then when people pick there position they would like to play then if there is a conflict someone can move them to a different position.

    What can an admin see?

    He can see everyone who signed up and the positions they would like to play. They can see everything a player would like to play but has the finally say in which position the person can play
